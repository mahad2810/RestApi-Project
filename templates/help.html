Help & Documentation

Getting Started

Welcome to Trade2Algo, a powerful trading platform designed for both novice and experienced traders. This guide will help you navigate the platform and make the most of its features.

Login

To start using Trade2Algo, enter your credentials on the login page. If you don't have an account, please contact our support team.

Navigation

Use the navigation bar at the top of the screen to access different sections of the platform:

  • Place Order: Submit new orders for various instruments
  • Order Book: View and manage your pending and executed orders
  • Trade Book: Check your trade history
  • Positions Book: Monitor your current positions and P&L

Placing Orders

To place a new order:

  1. Navigate to the Place Order section
  2. Select the exchange (e.g., NSE_CM, NSE_FO)
  3. Choose the instrument type (EQ for equities, OPTIDX for index options)
  4. Select the symbol (e.g., RELIANCE, NIFTY)
  5. For options, specify option type, expiry date, and strike price
  6. Select transaction type (BUY or SELL)
  7. Choose the product type (NRML)
  8. Select order type (L for limit, MKT for market)
  9. Enter quantity
  10. For limit orders, enter price
  11. Click "Place Order" to submit

Monitoring Orders

The Order Book displays all your orders with their current status:

  • Complete: Order has been fully executed
  • Pending: Order is waiting to be executed
  • Rejected: Order was rejected (hover over the reason field for details)
  • Canceled: Order was canceled by you or the system

Click the "Refresh" button to update the order status.

Trade Book

The Trade Book shows all your executed trades. You can sort the trades by clicking on any column header.

Positions Book

The Positions Book displays your current positions with real-time P&L. The summary section at the bottom shows your total profit, total loss, and net P&L.
